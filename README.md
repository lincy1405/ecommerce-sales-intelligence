# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Intelligence

An end-to-end business analysis project on 1M+ rows of real UK e-commerce transaction data (2009–2011).

## 🔗 Live Dashboard
**👉 [Click here to view the live dashboard](https://lincy-ecommerce-intelligence.streamlit.app/)**

---

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which countries drive the most revenue — and are they also most profitable?
2. What does month-over-month customer retention look like, and when do we lose them?
3. Which products have the highest return rate — should we delist them?
4. What does customer segmentation look like using RFM analysis?
5. When should we run promotions — best time of year and day of week?

---

## Key Insights
| # | Insight |
|---|---------|
| 1 | Netherlands has the highest AOV at £2,430 — 5x higher than Germany |
| 2 | 29.6% of customers are Champions but 1,155 are At Risk of churning |
| 3 | Revenue peaks in Nov — promotions should be moved to October |
| 4 | Thursday 10am–3pm is the best time to run campaigns |
| 5 | One bulk order of 80,995 units skews product data — B2B vs B2C split needed |
